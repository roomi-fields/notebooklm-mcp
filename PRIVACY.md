# Privacy Policy — notebooklm

_Last updated: 2026-06-28_

This plugin automates Google NotebookLM on your behalf.

- **Data collected by the plugin author:** none. The plugin contains no telemetry
  or analytics and sends no data to the author.
- **Credentials:** the plugin uses your own Google account session(s).
  Authentication tokens/cookies are stored locally on your machine and are used
  only to drive NotebookLM. They are never transmitted to the author.
- **Data sent to Google:** your questions, prompts, and any source content you
  submit are sent to Google NotebookLM so the plugin can function. That data is
  processed by Google under Google's Privacy Policy
  (https://policies.google.com/privacy). This plugin is independent and is not
  affiliated with or endorsed by Google.
- **Local data:** answers and downloaded artifacts are written to your local
  machine.

**Contact:** https://github.com/roomi-fields/notebooklm-mcp/issues
